Les Rougon-Macquart

If you got here, you probably already know who/what the Rougon-Macquart are. If you don't, here is a little recap.
Rougon-Macquart is a family saga composed by 20 books.
The family tree origina tes from Tante Dide who had a child with Rougon and two children with Macquart.
Those children and grand-children are featured in the books, where Zola can show how the genes can determine choices of life and faults.
The family tree is often featured in the introduction of books. Zola himself drew thos family tree, the final version after the release of the last book of the saga, Le Docteur Pascal.
